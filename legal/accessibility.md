# Accessibility Statement

CoinPort is committed to making our platform accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We aim to provide an inclusive experience that meets the needs of the widest possible audience.

## Our Commitment

We aim to conform to the [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG22/) at Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, cognitive, and motor impairments.

## Current Status

We are actively working to improve the accessibility of our platform. At this time, parts of our service substantially conform to WCAG 2.2 Level AA, while other areas are under active remediation. Known focus areas include:

- **Colour contrast** on secondary text and status indicators
- **Keyboard accessibility** for complex components such as trading charts and order books
- **Responsive reflow** on narrow viewports
- **Consistent heading structure** across authenticated pages

We are committed to resolving these gaps in upcoming releases.

## Measures We Take

- We include accessibility considerations in our design and development processes.
- We provide a "Skip to main content" link on every page so keyboard and screen reader users can bypass repeated navigation.
- We use semantic HTML landmarks (`<main>`, `<nav>`, `<header>`, `<footer>`) so assistive technology can navigate our site efficiently.
- We provide accessible names for icon-only controls, such as the menu toggle and theme switcher.
- We maintain visible keyboard focus indicators across interactive controls.
- We support browser zoom and text resizing.

## Compatibility

CoinPort is designed to be compatible with recent versions of major browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and commonly used assistive technologies, including screen readers such as VoiceOver, NVDA and JAWS.

## Feedback and Contact

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of CoinPort. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, or if you have suggestions on how we can improve, please contact us:

- **Email:** [support@coinport.com.au](mailto:support@coinport.com.au)
- **Subject line:** "Accessibility feedback"

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within five business days.

## Formal Complaints

If you are not satisfied with our response, you can follow our [Complaints Process](/legal/complaints). In addition, the [Australian Human Rights Commission](https://humanrights.gov.au/complaints) can receive complaints about unlawful discrimination under the *Disability Discrimination Act 1992* (Cth).

## Ongoing Review

This statement is reviewed and updated as the platform evolves. We welcome independent accessibility testing and audit feedback from our community.
