Job Title: Marketing Specialist

Company:

Coin Habour Pty Ltd

Location:

Melbourne, Australia. (remote working)

About Us:

CoinPort is a new Australian cryptocurrency exchange committed to providing a secure, user-friendly, and innovative platform for buying, selling, and trading cryptocurrencies. As the cryptocurrency market continues to evolve, we are looking for a passionate and dynamic Marketing Specialist to join our team and help drive our brand's growth and recognition in the industry.

Job Description:

Position Overview:

We are seeking a Marketing Specialist to join our team and take charge of developing and executing marketing strategies that will position Coin Habour Pty Ltd as a trusted and reliable cryptocurrency exchange in the Australian market. The ideal candidate will have a deep understanding of the cryptocurrency industry and be well-versed in digital marketing techniques, branding, and customer acquisition strategies.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Digital Marketing Strategy: Develop and implement a comprehensive digital marketing strategy to increase brand awareness, drive website traffic, and acquire new users.
  • Content Creation: Create high-quality, engaging, and informative content for various digital channels, including social media, blogs, email marketing, and video.
  • Social Media Management: Manage and grow our social media presence on platforms such as Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Instagram. Monitor trends, engage with the community, and execute effective social media campaigns.
  • SEO & SEM: Optimize website content for search engines (SEO) and manage search engine marketing (SEM) campaigns to improve organic and paid search visibility.
  • Email Marketing: Develop and execute email marketing campaigns to nurture leads, retain customers, and promote new services and features.
  • Community Engagement: Build and maintain relationships with the cryptocurrency community, including traders, enthusiasts, and influencers. Foster a positive brand image through engagement and communication.
  • Analytics & Reporting: Monitor and analyze marketing performance metrics to assess the effectiveness of campaigns. Use data-driven insights to make informed decisions and adjustments to marketing strategies.
  • Partnerships: Identify and collaborate with relevant industry partners and influencers to expand the reach and credibility of Coin Habour Pty Ltd.
  • Branding: Ensure consistent branding across all marketing materials and platforms, maintaining a strong and recognizable company image.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business, or a related field.
  • 3+ years of experience in digital marketing, preferably in the cryptocurrency or fintech industry.
  • Proven track record of developing and executing successful digital marketing campaigns.
  • Strong understanding of cryptocurrency and blockchain technology.
  • Proficiency in SEO, SEM, social media marketing, and email marketing.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Data-driven mindset with the ability to analyze and interpret marketing metrics.
  • Creative thinking and the ability to stay up-to-date with industry trends.
  • Team player with strong interpersonal skills.
  • Graphic design skills, including video production for Youtube, Tiktok etc
  • Ability to adapt to a fast-paced and evolving industry.

Benefits:

  • Opportunities for career growth and development.
  • A collaborative and dynamic work environment.
  • Access to cutting-edge cryptocurrency technology and insights.
