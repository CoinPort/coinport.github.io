Job Title: Help Desk Support Staff - Australian Cryptocurrency Exchange

Location:

Melbourne, Australia. (remote working)

Employment Type:

Full-time

Company Overview:

Coin Habour Pty Ltd is a leading Australian cryptocurrency exchange dedicated to providing secure and user-friendly cryptocurrency trading services to our customers. We are committed to promoting the adoption of blockchain technology and ensuring a seamless experience for our users.

Job Summary:

We are seeking a dedicated and customer-focused Help Desk Support Staff to join our dynamic team. As a Help Desk Support Staff member, you will play a crucial role in ensuring our customers receive prompt and effective assistance with their cryptocurrency-related queries and technical issues. You will serve as the first point of contact for our users, offering expert guidance and troubleshooting assistance to ensure a positive customer experience.

Key Responsibilities:

Customer Support:

  • Provide timely and friendly support to customers via various channels, including email, chat, and phone.
  • Address inquiries related to account setup, trading, deposits, withdrawals, and general cryptocurrency-related questions.
  • Assist customers in navigating our platform, ensuring they have a smooth experience.
  • Resolve customer issues efficiently, escalating complex cases to the appropriate department when necessary.

Technical Troubleshooting:

  • Diagnose and troubleshoot technical issues that customers may encounter while using our platform.
  • Guide customers through the resolution process, including password resets, two-factor authentication setup, and wallet management.
  • Collaborate with the technical team to ensure prompt resolution of complex technical problems.

Education and Training:

  • Educate customers about cryptocurrency trading best practices, security measures, and regulatory compliance.
  • Conduct training sessions or webinars to enhance customers' understanding of cryptocurrency trading and our platform.

Documentation:

  • Maintain a comprehensive knowledge base and FAQs to assist customers in finding answers to common questions.
  • Create user-friendly guides and documentation for various processes and procedures.

Compliance and Security:

  • Testing and Quality Assurance
  • Document and help resolve customer enquiires using Freshdesk.
  • Adhere to all company policies and regulatory guidelines, ensuring customers' security and compliance with Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) procedures.

Qualifications:

  • Prior experience in a customer support role, preferably in the cryptocurrency or fintech industry.
  • Strong knowledge of cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology.
  • Excellent communication skills with the ability to explain complex technical concepts to non-technical users.
  • Proficiency in using support software and tools, as well as the ability to quickly learn new systems.
  • High level of empathy and patience in dealing with customer inquiries and issues.
  • Problem-solving skills and the ability to work under pressure in a fast-paced environment.
  • Familiarity with Australian cryptocurrency regulations and compliance is a plus.

Benefits:

  • Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
  • Opportunities for career growth and development within the cryptocurrency industry.
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and retirement benefits.
  • A collaborative and innovative work environment.
  • Regular team-building activities and social events.
