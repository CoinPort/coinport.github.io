# Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) Materials

This folder stores the customer-facing and staff-facing materials used as part of CoinPort's Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) process under our AML/CTF Program, aimed at protecting new and high-risk customers — particularly older customers and those in rural or regional areas — from scams.

[Scam Awareness Email (Markdown source)](EDD-email.md) — Source content for the scam-awareness email sent to new customers before their first payment, covering common scam types (romance/pig butchering, fake investments, recovery room scams, impersonation, job/task scams, remote access scams) and a checklist of questions to ask before sending money.

[Scam Awareness Email (HTML)](EDD-email.html) — HTML-formatted version of the same email, ready to paste into an email service provider for sending to customers.

[Scam Awareness Email (PDF)](EDD-email.pdf) — PDF export of the email, for sharing as an attachment or download link.

[EDD Phone Script](EDD-phone-script.md) — Script used by staff when calling high-risk customers to assess whether they may be a scam victim before a transaction is processed, including targeted questions, vulnerability-sensitive guidance, escalation steps, and record-keeping requirements.
