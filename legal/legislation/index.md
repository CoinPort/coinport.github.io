## Pending Legislation

- **Corporations Amendment (Digital Assets Framework) Bill 2025** — Introduced 26 Nov 2025, referred to Senate Economics Legislation Committee 5 Feb 2026. Not yet enacted. Would require Digital Asset Platform operators to hold an AFSL. [Track progress](https://www.aph.gov.au/Parliamentary_Business/Bills_Legislation/Bills_Search_Results/Result?bId=r7411)

## Notes

- The AML/CTF Amendment Act 2024 replaced the term "digital currency exchange" with **"virtual asset service provider (VASP)"** and "digital currency" with **"virtual asset"**, aligning with FATF Recommendation 15.
- The Financial Transaction Reports Act 1988 was repealed on 7 January 2025 — all AML/CTF obligations are now consolidated under the AML/CTF Act 2006.
