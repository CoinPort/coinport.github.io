We have compiled details of competitors for research, analysis and benchmarking purposes.

Our expectation is that the recent announcement by (Federal) The Department of Treasury and Finance that Digital Asset Exchanges in Australia will require an AFSL will act as a signifiance "barrier to entry" for new potential competitors and force consolidation in the sector.
