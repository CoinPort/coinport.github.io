Here are several highly-regarded New Zealand law firms with dedicated expertise in financial services regulation, fintech, and cryptocurrency, making them well-suited to assist CoinPort Limited with FSP registration and related compliance.

### **Top-Tier Full-Service Firms (with Dedicated Fintech/Financial Services Teams)**

These firms offer comprehensive expertise and are accustomed to dealing with complex, cross-border structures like yours.

1.  **Chapman Tripp**
    *   **Why they're relevant:** Consistently ranked as New Zealand's leading commercial law firm. They have a premier **Financial Services Regulation team** and a dedicated **Digital Assets & Cryptocurrency practice**. They advise major banks, fintechs, and crypto platforms on licensing, AML/CFT, and regulatory strategy. They are excellent for high-stakes, complex matters.
    *   **Key Contact:** Look for partners in their **Financial Services Regulation** or **Technology & Innovation** groups in Auckland or Wellington.
    *   **Website:** [chapmantripp.com](https://www.chapmantripp.com)

2.  **Buddle Findlay**
    *   **Why they're relevant:** Another top-tier firm with a strong focus on financial markets and fintech. Their **Financial Services team** regularly advises on FSPR registration, AML/CFT, and the Financial Markets Conduct Act. They are pragmatic and have deep experience with both established institutions and innovative startups.
    *   **Key Contact:** Partners in their **Banking and Financial Services** practice in Auckland or Wellington.
    *   **Website:** [buddlefindlay.com](https://www.buddlefindlay.com)

3.  **MinterEllisonRuddWatts**
    *   **Why they're relevant:** A major firm with a dedicated **Fintech sector leadership**. Their **Financial Services regulation team** handles the full spectrum of licensing, compliance, and corporate structuring for digital asset businesses. They are known for being commercially focused.
    *   **Key Contact:** Partners in the **Financial Services** or **Corporate** teams.
    *   **Website:** [minterellison.co.nz](https://www.minterellison.co.nz)

### **Specialist and Highly Recommended Boutique/Disruptor Firms**

These firms are often more nimble and may offer more tailored, partner-led service for a growing business like yours.

Their **Digital Assets and Financial Services teams** work closely together. They have been active in the crypto space for years and understand the practical challenges of exchange operation.
    *   **Key Contact:** Partners in the **Financial Services Regulation** or **Technology and Digital** practices.

They can be a very good balance of expertise and relative cost-effectiveness compared to the very top tier.

They act for a range of payment processors, fintech companies, and financial institutions. Their size often allows for direct, efficient partner involvement.

7.  **Fee Langstone**
    *   **Why they're relevant:** An Auckland-based commercial firm with a niche in **financial services and regulatory compliance**. They are often recommended for FSPR and AML/CFT work and are known for being very hands-on and practical.
    *   **Key Contact:** Partners in their **Financial Services** practice.
    *   **Website:** [feelangstone.co.nz](https://www.feelangstone.co.nz)

### **Key Advisory/Corporate Trustee Firms (For "Controller" Role)**

While not law firms, these are critical professional services you will likely need. They often work alongside your legal counsel.

*   **Guardian Trust** and **Perpetual Guardian**: These established firms specialize in providing independent **Director, Trustee, and "Controller" services**. They can fulfill the mandatory NZ-resident Controller role for your FSPR application, bringing regulatory experience and independence.
*   **Professional Trustee Services:** A corporate trustee firm that is very active in the fintech space, providing governance, controller, and AML/CFT officer services.

### **How to Proceed: Recommendations for CoinPort**

1.  **Shortlist:** Identify 2-3 firms from the list above that seem to best match your needs in terms of specialty, size, and likely cost structure.
2.  **Initial Inquiry:** Reach out via their website contact forms or direct email to a relevant partner. Be clear in your inquiry:
    *   "We are **CoinPort Limited**, a NZ subsidiary of an Australian cryptocurrency exchange, seeking to register as a Financial Service Provider ('value transfer service') and establish full AML/CFT compliance."
    *   Request a brief introductory call or a scope of work/proposal.
3.  **Discuss Structure:** In your discussions, explicitly ask about:
    *   Their experience with crypto exchange FSP registrations.
    *   Their approach to the **"Controller" requirement** (can they recommend a provider, or work with one you identify?).
    *   A bundled approach to handle **FSPR registration, AML/CFT programme drafting, and FIU registration**.
    *   Estimated costs and timelines.

**Final Advice:** Given your Australian parentage, **Chapman Tripp** or **Buddle Findlay** are particularly experienced with Trans-Tasman structures. For a potentially more tailored and cost-conscious approach, **K3 Legal** or **Fee Langstone** are excellent specialist choices.

It is highly advisable to secure New Zealand legal advice before submitting any applications to ensure your structure and documentation are robust from the start.
