Target market

Our target market is Australian retail investors and traders. Initially individuals, later companies and Self-Managed Super Funds.

At some point in the future will will consider expanding into overseas markets, once well established in the Australian market and when adequate resources are available. We have identified New Zealand, Malaysia and Thailand as prospective markets.

We will focus on new entrants to the market, rather than experts who might have accounts with many exchanges. Our product offering is tailored to this market.

Market Forecast suggest 400% growth in market participants, and Coin Harbour is positioned to take advantage of this.
